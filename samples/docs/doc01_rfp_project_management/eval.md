## Expected Content Elements
- Title and issuing agency
- Project background / purpose
- Scope of work / services list (numbered)
- Compliance references to HOME/HTF or similar programs
- Payment / funding terms
- Submission instructions and deadlines
- Evaluation criteria (if present)

## Key Details
- RFP issued by a government housing authority for project management services on a HOME/HTF-funded housing project.
- Describes responsibilities such as grant administration, compliance reporting, procurement support, progress reporting, and project close-out.
- Clarifies that fees will be paid from grant funds and that some responsibilities (e.g., legal advice, audits, disbursal of funds) are explicitly excluded.
