# Getting Started

Busibox is a self-hosted AI platform that lets you upload documents, search them with natural language, and chat with AI agents that know your content. Your data stays on your organization's infrastructure.

## Logging In

You access Busibox through the AI Portal. Your administrator will give you the URL. When you open it, you can sign in using:

- **Passkey** — Use your device's built-in biometrics or security key
- **TOTP** — A one-time code from an authenticator app
- **Magic link** — A sign-in link sent to your email

Choose whichever method your organization has set up. No passwords required.

## The AI Portal Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard. This is your home base. From here you can:

- **Browse your documents** — See what you've uploaded and what's shared with you
- **Launch apps** — Open Agent Manager, Status Report, Estimator, and other tools
- **Access admin settings** — If you're an admin, manage users, ingestion, and deployments

The layout is simple: a sidebar for navigation, app cards on the home page, and quick access to documents and agents.

## Quick Tour: Upload, Search, Chat

Here's a fast way to see Busibox in action:

1. **Upload a document** — Go to Documents, click upload, and add a PDF, Word file, or other supported format. The system will process it automatically.
2. **Search it** — Once processing completes, use the search bar to ask questions in plain language. "What are the main findings?" or "Summarize the key points."
3. **Chat with an agent** — Open Agent Manager or the chat interface, enable document search, and ask the same questions. The agent will answer using your document and cite the source.

That's the core loop: upload, search, chat. Everything else builds on this.
